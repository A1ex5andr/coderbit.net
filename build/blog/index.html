18. September 2014

minimal-ui is removed from iOS 8

The minimal-ui viewport property is no longer supported in iOS 8

And I have to say that’s kinda sad. I really liked the behaviour of the minimal-ui property. Now its final.

It made landscape mode of browsing look so much cleaner. Especially when you work with a 100vh sections, so you don’t see browsers address bar on top, all the time.

Well, maybe there will be a better decision instead, soon :)
